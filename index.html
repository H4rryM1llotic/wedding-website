FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

PHONES AND PICTURES?
We would love for you to take photos and videos throughout the day. However, we kindly ask that no phones are present in the ceremony, and no photos or videos are taken. We also kindly ask that no photos or videos of the couple are posted on social media until they have had the chance to do so themselves.
CAN I BRING MY CHILDREN?
As much as we would like to accomodate every child, we only have space for a select few.
CAN I BRING A PLUS ONE?
No, only those named on the invitation are invited.
CAN I BRING CONFETTI?
No, confetti will be supplied at the venue.
CAN I PARK AT THE VENUE?
Parking at the venue is limited, and as such is reserved for guests staying at the venue. There are plenty car parks close by.
ACCOMODATION?
There are plenty of hotels in the city of Chester, and a few closer to the venue: Hotel Indigo, Travelodge Chester Central, Leonardo Hotel Chester.
